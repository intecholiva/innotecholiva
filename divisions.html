Strategic Divisions

Dedicated team delivering tailored solutions across every stage of virgin olive oil value chain.

An Integrated Approach to Excellence

At INNOTECHOLIVA, we structure our work through specialized strategic divisions, each focused on a key area of the virgin olive oil value chain. This integrated approach allows us to provide targeted, high-value support to producers, cooperatives, and institutions—from extraction and quality control to branding, certification, and international expansion. Our multidisciplinary team combines technical expertise with market insight to drive innovation, ensure excellence, and promote sustainable growth.

Technologies, Innovation & Process Optimization

We advise producers and cooperatives on technological optimization of the extraction process, blending tradition with innovation. We assess and improve mill performance, guide equipment investments, and train staff in operational efficiency.

Quality Management & Sensory Analysis

Our experts implement international quality standards (IOC, EU), train sensory panels, and help identify and eliminate off-flavors. We assist clients in maintaining exceptional organoleptic profiles and gaining market recognition.

Certification & Traceability

We guide clients through PDO/PGI certifications, sustainability labeling (e.g., organic, carbon footprint), and digital traceability systems (blockchain, QR). This builds consumer trust and brand transparency.

Branding & Product Development

Our team supports the development of high-differentiation EVOO brands. We craft identity, packaging, storytelling, and sensory positioning strategies aligned with market trends and premium expectations.

Internationalization & Export Strategies

We assist producers and institutions in navigating foreign markets, from regulatory compliance and partner search to tailored entry plans. We help align your product with international consumer preferences and market gaps.

Sustainability & Circular Economy

We design circular economy strategies for by-product valorization, water reuse, and CO₂ footprint reduction. Our focus is on long-term environmental and economic sustainability in olive oil ecosystems.

Innovation & R&D Partnerships

We facilitate collaboration with universities, research centers, and startups to foster innovation in extraction, health claims, AI-based traceability, and more. We help clients stay ahead through science and technology.

Be part of the evolution of EVOO—driven by innovation and sustainability.
