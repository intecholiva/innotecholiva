About INNOTECHOLIVA

Driving innovation and excellence in the olive oil industry with a commitment to quality, sustainability, and global impact.

Our Story

Fusing Science with Strategy

INNOTECHOLIVA is a strategic consulting firm specializing in the virgin olive oil sector. Our multidisciplinary team merges scientific expertise, technological insight, and international market strategies to support producers, cooperatives, and institutions in maximizing value across the entire olive oil value chain. We are passionate about transforming challenges into opportunities for growth and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a global leader in strategic innovation for the olive oil industry, fostering excellence, transparency, and sustainable growth worldwide.

Our Mission

To provide expert consulting that empowers stakeholders in the olive oil ecosystem through tailored strategies, innovation, and international positioning.

Meet the Founder

Dr. Abdelaziz Boudebouz

With over 15 years of experience in agrotechnology and a Ph.D. in Food Science specialized in Olive science and oil technology, Dr. Boudebouz founded INNOTECHOLIVA to bridge the gap between scientific research and practical application in the olive oil industry. His vision is to empower producers with the knowledge and tools to create world-class products sustainably.
