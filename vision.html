Our Visions

Guiding the extra virgin olive oil sector toward innovation, sustainability, and global leadership.

Empowering Excellence in Extra Virgin Olive Oil

At INNOTECHOLIVA, our vision is to be the reference in strategic consulting for the extra virgin olive oil sector. We aim to empower producers, cooperatives, and institutions by transforming the value chain with innovation, sustainability, and scientific excellence.

We believe in an olive oil industry that is not only profitable but also environmentally responsible and deeply rooted in quality.

Our Approach

Our Commitment is to:

Promote cutting-edge technological solutions in olive oil production.

Foster eco-friendly practices that ensure long-term sustainability.

Support branding and market differentiation strategies to enhance value.

Provide access to international markets through strategic positioning.

Encourage knowledge transfer and collaborative innovation.

International Trainings & Strategic Advisory.

By aligning tradition with future-focused thinking, we strive to position extra virgin olive oil as a global benchmark of excellence, health, and sustainability.
